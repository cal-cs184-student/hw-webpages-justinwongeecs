Task 3: Transforms

What is being done to the robot

The robot is modified to have both arms raised over its head into a celebratory pose. Transformations were applied on the “upper arms”, rotating the blocks to translate the blocks slightly outwards and rotating them upwards to simulate shoulder movement. Then, upward translations and upward rotations were applied on the “lower arms” to simulate bending at the elbows to put the arms up. Since SVG transformations are hierarchical, the lower arms move in relation to how the upper arms move, so it was necessary to make adjustments accordingly to make sure the transformations achieve the desired visual effect.Transformations were performed on one side first before mirroring them across the vertical axis in order to maintain symmetry.
